Save the Children aims to uphold children's rights and to ensure hat children can be children, wherever they are.

Trust and support us, because:

  • We support children from all around the world, providing urgent help to those in need of it, including those facing natural calamity and war. Save the Children Philippines has been at the forefront of humanitarian and emergency responses and continue to help children recover from the impacts of calamityand armed conflict in the Philippines.
  • Through our work, we have reached more that 183 million children worldwide by the end of 2021, and in 2021 alone, we've reached 4.4 million Filipinos.
  • Save the Children aims to protect children from various forms of abuse and exploitation, such as online sexual abuse, illegal drugs, and armed conflict.
